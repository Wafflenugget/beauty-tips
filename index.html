Skincare Instructions

Follow these steps for a healthy skincare routine:

Step 1: Cleanse

Start by washing your face with a gentle cleanser to remove dirt, oil, and makeup. Use lukewarm water and avoid harsh scrubbing.

Step 2: Exfoliate

Exfoliate your skin 2-3 times a week to remove dead skin cells. Choose a gentle exfoliator suitable for your skin type.

Step 3: Tone

Apply a toner to balance your skin's pH levels and remove any remaining impurities. Use a cotton pad or your hands to gently pat the toner onto your skin.

Step 4: Moisturize

Moisturize your skin to keep it hydrated. Choose a moisturizer that suits your skin type and apply it evenly to your face and neck.

Step 5: Protect

Apply sunscreen with at least SPF 30 every morning, even on cloudy days. Sunscreen protects your skin from harmful UV rays and prevents premature aging.

Remember to repeat this routine every day for best results!
